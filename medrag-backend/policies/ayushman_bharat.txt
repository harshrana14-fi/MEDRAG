Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)
Coverage: Rs. 5 Lakh per family per year.
Eligibility: Families listed in the SECC database.
Waiting Period: Zero waiting period for pre-existing diseases.
Services: Covers hospitalization, surgery, diagnostic tests, and medications.
Exclusions: Cosmetic surgeries, fertility treatments, and outpatient care (OPD) are generally not covered.
Claim Process: Cashless treatment at empanelled hospitals.
Required Documents: Aadhaar card or Ration card for identification.
