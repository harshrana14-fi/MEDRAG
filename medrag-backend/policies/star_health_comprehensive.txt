Star Health Comprehensive Insurance Policy
Waiting Periods:
- 30 days initial waiting period.
- 24 months for specific diseases (Cataract, Hernia, etc.).
- 48 months for pre-existing diseases.
Co-payment: 20% co-payment for persons above 61 years of age entry.
Benefits: 
- Hospitalization cover (Room, ICU, Docs).
- Pre and Post hospitalization (60 and 90 days).
- Delivery expenses cover.
Exclusions: 
- Self-inflicted injury.
- Alcoholism or drug abuse.
- Cosmetic procedures.
Claim Types: 
- Cashless: Network hospitals.
- Reimbursement: Non-network hospitals.
Required Documents: Discharge summary, original medical bills, claim form, and identity proof.
